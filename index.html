How Online Architecture Works

A clear, structured digital workflow from consultation to execution-ready drawings

01

Discovery Call

We begin with an online consultation to understand your requirements, lifestyle, site details, and project goals.

02

Site Data & Analysis

Site dimensions, photographs, and local survey data are shared digitally and analyzed to form the design foundation.

03

Concept & 3D Design

We develop concept layouts and 3D visualizations, allowing you to experience the space before construction begins.

04

Design Development

Feedback-driven refinements ensure the design aligns perfectly with functionality, aesthetics, and budget.

05

Technical Drawings

Detailed construction drawings are delivered, ready for execution by your local contractor or builder.

06

Remote Support

We stay connected during execution through calls and reviews to ensure design intent is maintained on site.
