Private Residence

Built Project · Residential Architecture

Project Overview

This private residence was designed as a calm, functional home that balances spatial clarity with minimal architectural expression. The design focuses on natural light, ventilation, and long-term usability.

Design Approach

The planning strategy emphasizes clean geometry, efficient circulation, and seamless indoor–outdoor connections. Materials were selected to ensure durability and timeless aesthetics.
